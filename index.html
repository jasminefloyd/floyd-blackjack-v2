Blackjack Rules

  • Goal: Get 21 or higher than the dealer without going over 21.
  • Cards: Numbers are their value, face cards = 10, Ace = 1 or 11
  • Chips: Each player will start at $300 chips. Minimum bet is $5. Game is 2:1
  • Player's Turn: Tap "Hit" for another card or "Stay" to keep your total
  • Dealer: Must draw until their hand is 17 or higher
  • To Win: If you're dealt 21, BLACKJACK!! - you instantly win! If you're closer to 21 than the dealer, or if the dealer busts (goes over 21)
